Welcome to DeepFake Detection Program

A deepfake video is a type of synthetic media that has been created or altered using deep learning techniques, particularly generative adversarial networks (GANs). These videos typically involve the manipulation of facial expressions and movements to superimpose the likeness of one person onto another person's body or face, creating a realistic but entirely fake video.
